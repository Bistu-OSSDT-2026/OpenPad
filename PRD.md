# PRD

## Product

OpenPad Lab is a browser-based pad sampler for beginners and course demos. It combines sample upload, chopping, pads, a 16-step sequencer, FX controls, gesture mapping, and project JSON save/load.

## MVP

- 4x4 pads with mouse and keyboard triggering.
- Audio upload and basic waveform area.
- Chop into 4, 8, or 16 equal slices.
- Assign chopped samples to pads.
- 16-step sequencer with BPM and play/stop.
- At least two audible FX controls.
- Gesture input maps to FX parameters, not direct note triggering.
- Export and import project JSON.

## Non-goals

- Full DAW timeline.
- SP-404 clone or Roland branding.
- Real-time multi-user collaboration.
- Automatic BPM detection.
- WAV export in P0.
